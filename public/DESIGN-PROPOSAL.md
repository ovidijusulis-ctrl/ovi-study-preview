# Ovi English School - Design Proposal

## Design Philosophy
**"Premium but Non-Distracting"**

Clean, sophisticated, and focused on learning. The design should feel like a premium educational platform — trustworthy and professional — without flashy or distracting elements.

---

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Navy** (`#1a2f4a`) - Trust, professionalism
- **Warm Cream** (`#f8f5f0`) - Easy on eyes, paper-like reading

### Accent Colors
- **Soft Gold** (`#c9a962`) - Premium feel, highlights
- **Muted Teal** (`#4a8b8b`) - Calm, focus

### Text Colors
- **Charcoal** (`#2d2d2d`) - Primary text (softer than black)
- **Slate Gray** (`#6b7280`) - Secondary text

---

## 2. Typography

### Headings
**Font:** Playfair Display (Serif)
- Elegant, premium feel
- Distinctive but readable

### Body Text
**Font:** Source Sans 3 (Sans-serif)
- Clean, highly readable for long-form content
- Good for study materials

### Sizes
- H1: 2.5rem (40px)
- H2: 1.75rem (28px)
- Body: 1.125rem (18px) - larger for readability
- Line height: 1.7 for comfortable reading

---

## 3. Key Design Elements

### Header
- Clean navigation
- Logo with tagline
- Language selector (minimal)

### Hero Section (Homepage)
- Large, welcoming heading
- Brief value proposition
- "Start Learning" CTA button
- Subtle background pattern

### Episode Cards (Archive)
- Thumbnail image
- Title + short description
- Level badge (Beginner/Intermediate)
- Topic tag
- Clean hover effect

### Episode Page
- Audio player (prominent, beautiful)
- Tabbed transcript (Study/Listen/Reading)
- Interactive word highlighting
- Progress indicators for flashcards/quiz

---

## 4. Layout Principles

- **Generous whitespace** - Prevents clutter
- **Single column for reading** - Max-width 720px for content
- **Consistent spacing** - 8px grid system
- **Subtle shadows** - Depth without heaviness
- **Smooth transitions** - 0.2s ease for interactions

---

1. **Typography & Colors** - Quick win, huge impact
